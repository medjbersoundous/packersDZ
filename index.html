Packers Delivery FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our delivery services, coverage, payment options, and tracking. Contact us for further assistance.

Who are we?

Packers Delivery is a company based in Aïn Naadja, Algiers, offering reliable delivery services across all wilayas with cash-on-delivery options.

Where are Packers located?

Our main office is located in Aïn Naadja, Algiers, near the metro station, with additional locations in Boumerdes, Tipaza, Blida, and Medea.

Does delivery cover all wilayas?

Yes, Packers Delivery provides comprehensive coverage to all 58 wilayas in Algeria.

Do Packers recover payment from my customers?

Yes, our cash-on-delivery service is available nationwide, and we handle collecting the payment for goods directly from your customers.

How can I check the progress of my parcels?

We provide a real-time tracking platform that allows you to monitor the status of your orders and funds.

Are deliveries made to homes or Packers' offices?

We offer two delivery options:
Doorstep Delivery with Cash on Delivery: Your order is delivered to your chosen address, and our distributor will contact you to confirm delivery.
Office Delivery with Cash on Delivery: Your order is delivered to our company office, and you will be notified to pick it up upon arrival.

Does Packers provide pick-up service?

Yes, Packers offers a free pick-up service for your convenience.

What is the cost of return?

Our return service is provided free of charge across all 58 wilayas.

Pricing and Delivery Policies

The following policies outline critical details for calculating delivery costs and managing shipments. Please review these terms to ensure accurate pricing and timely delivery.

Return Shipping

Return shipping is provided at no cost for all wilayas.

Weight-Based Pricing

Pricing applies to parcels up to 10 kg. An additional 50 DZD per kilogram is charged for weight exceeding 10 kg.

Volume-Based Pricing

Volume is calculated as (Length × Width × Height) × 200. The higher value between weight and volume determines the pricing.

Delivery Duration

Delivery duration is calculated from the date the parcel is received at the sending office. Fridays are excluded from the delivery duration.

Return Collection

Returned parcels must be collected within 7 days of arrival at the office. A storage fee of 20 DZD per day is applied thereafter.
